Welcome to Stockholm Bed & Breakfast

The Stockholm Bed & Breakfast the right choice for visitors who are searching for a combination of charm, peace and quiet, and a convenient position from which to explore Stockholm. It is a small, comfortable hotel, situated in Värtahamnen, close to the subway station - Gärdet. The buffet breakfast is served in the lounge on the ground floor, and also outside on our little patio during the summer months. The hotel provides a free Wi-Fi service.
